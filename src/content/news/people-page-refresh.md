The People page received a structural refresh focused on clearer profile cards, stronger visual consistency, and more reliable image handling.

The update also improves how visitors move between the People page and related sections such as Projects and Publications. That makes the site more useful for collaborators, prospective students, and anyone trying to understand who contributes to different parts of the lab's research program.

Future updates can build on this foundation by adding richer news items, event recaps, and profile-linked announcements.
