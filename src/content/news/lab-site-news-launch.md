We launched a dedicated News section to make it easier to share updates from the Systems Structural Biology Group in one place.

This section is designed for short announcements, publication highlights, project milestones, team achievements, event recaps, and recruiting notices. Instead of burying updates across static pages, the site can now support a clear, time-ordered stream of activity.

As the site evolves, this section can also serve as a lightweight public archive for the lab's progress over time.
