Recent site updates added more research visuals to help explain the lab's work in structural biology, molecular dynamics, and multiscale discovery.

These visuals make it easier for visitors, collaborators, and prospective students to understand both the scientific scope of the group and the kinds of computational questions the lab studies. They also provide a stronger bridge between technical results and public-facing communication.

This approach will continue across future pages so that publications, projects, and news can connect more clearly through shared visual context.
